Okroshka Recipe:

Description:

The recipe is very easy and not complicated at all

The ingredients used are very simple and time to prepare that dish is about 1 hour

Ingredients:

  • Cucumber
  • Redish
  • Potato
  • Dill
  • Eggs
  • Kolbasa/Meat
  • Kefir
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lemon juice
  • Smetana

Steps:

  1. Cut all into small cubes
  2. Put all in the pot
  3. Add water, kefir and smetana
  4. tadam ready!
